P101. Let $a, b, c > 0$ such that $abc = 8$. Determine the largest constant $C$ such that the following inequality holds for all $a, b, c$:

$$
\frac{a^2}{\sqrt{\left(1+a^3\right)\left(1+b^3\right)}}+\frac{b^2}{\sqrt{\left(1+b^3\right)\left(1+c^3\right)}}+\frac{c^2}{\sqrt{\left(1+c^3\right)\left(1+a^3\right)}} \geq C.
$$

P102. Let $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n > 0$ such that $a_1 + a_2 + \ldots + a_n < 1$. Determine the minimal constant $C$ such that the following inequality holds for all $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n$:

$$
\frac{a_1 \cdot a_2 \ldots a_n \left(1 - a_1 - a_2 - \ldots - a_n\right)}{\left(a_1 + a_2 + \ldots + a_n\right)\left(1-a_1\right)\left(1-a_2\right) \ldots \left(1-a_n\right)} \leq C\frac{3}{n^{n-1}}.
$$
