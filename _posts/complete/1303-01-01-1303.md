**P401.** Let $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n$ be positive real numbers with

$$
a_1 \ge a_2 \ge \cdots \ge a_n.
$$

$$
\left(\frac{a_1+a_2}{2}\right)\left(\frac{a_2+a_3}{2}\right)\cdots\left(\frac{a_n+a_1}{2}\right)

**P402.** Autocorrelation Inequality

Let $$f \ge 0$$ and suppose the support of $$f$$ is contained in $$[-\tfrac14,\tfrac14]$$. Assume that, for **all** such functions $$f$$, the following inequality holds:

$$
\max_{|t|\le \tfrac12} (f * f)(t) \ge C_1\left(\int f\right)^2.
$$

Find an **upper bound** for $$C_1$$. *Note: This is essentially a “min–max” problem (minimizing the worst-case / largest value).*

**A402.** The best currently known upper bound is

$$
C_1 \le 1.50286.
$$

**P403.** Autocorrelation Inequality

Let $$f \ge 0$$. Define

$$
C_2=\sup_{f\ge 0}\frac{\|f*f\|_2^2}{\|f*f\|_1\,\|f*f\|_\infty}.
$$

Find a **lower bound** for $$C_2$$. *Note: This is essentially about achieving a large value (maximization via the supremum).*

**A403.** The best currently known lower bound is

$$
C_2 \ge 0.9610.
$$
