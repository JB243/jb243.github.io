## 4. Other Categories

⑴ RNP (RNA-protein complex)

> ① Average human protein: 55 kDa. 1 protein. The protein surface area is approximately 19 nm².

> ② Average human mRNA: approximately 3 kilobases. More than 18 proteins are attached. The protein surface area is approximately 342 nm², and the RNA surface area is approximately 707 nm².

> ③ Very long noncoding mRNA: approximately 7 kilobases. More than 40 proteins are attached. The protein surface area is approximately 760 nm², and the RNA surface area is approximately 2,000 nm².
