## **2. Vector Space**

 ‚ë¥ Conditions for Vector Space: One of [algebraic structures](https://jb243.github.io/pages/2082#1-group-theory). ‚Ñù<sup>n</sup>, etc.

> ‚ë† Closed under addition: if x, y ‚àà V, x + y ‚àà V

> ‚ë° Closed under multiplication: ‚àÄa ‚àà ‚Ñù, ‚àÄx ‚àà V, ax ‚àà V

> ‚ë¢ Identity Element for Addition: There exists **0** in V such that **x** + **0** = **x** for any **x** in V

> ‚ë£ Additive Inverse: If **x** ‚àà V, there exists -**x** in V such that **x** + (-**x**) = **0**

> ‚ë§ Commutativity of Addition: If **x**, **y** ‚àà V, then **x** + **y** = **y** + **x**

> ‚ë• Associativity of Addition: If **x**, **y**, **z** ‚àà V, then (x + y) + z = x + (y + z)

> ‚ë¶ Distributive Law: c(**x** + **y**) = c**x** + c**y**

> ‚ëß Distributive Law: (c1 + c2)**x** = c1**x** + c2**x**

> ‚ë® Multiplicative Identity: 1**x** = **x**

> ‚ë© Associativity of Scalar Multiplication: c1(c2**x**) = (c1c2)**x**

 ‚ëµ Linear Transformation

> ‚ë† Linear Transformation: A mapping T: U ‚Üí V is linear if it satisfies the following for **x**, **y** ‚àà U and c ‚àà F
