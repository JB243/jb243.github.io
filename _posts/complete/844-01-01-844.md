 ○ For gift tax, bearing the tax burden is the general rule **:** however, for lineal family members, gifts can be made without tax up to KRW 50 million per 10 years

 ○ Stock capital gains tax applies to major shareholders with **≥ 1%** ownership or **≥ KRW 1 billion** (10억) in holdings

 ○ Securities transaction tax applies to “ants” (individual retail investors)
